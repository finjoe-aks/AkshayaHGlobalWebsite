Mission

Our aim is to create a guide in the form of a website, so we do not forget our knowledge in coding and also learn new things. We have written almost all the things we know and learnt about coding. Whenever we forget some tags or properties, we look through our own pages or the pages created by our friends.


Who we are

We are a group of 4 students studying in ninth grade. Each one of us is highly trained and experienced in programming. This website is the result of all our new and past experiences in html and css. Our website contains only CSS and HTML and no other front-end or back-end programming languages.


Will this help you?

We know that our website will help you in some way related to HTML, CSS and DBMS. If you ever forget some elements or units, you can check out our HTML, CSS and other menus. If you don't find the answer you're looking for, then leave a feedback and we'll look out for solutions. We keep updating our website, so a few facts that aren't available today might be available in the future.


Join us

We are available 24/7. You can reach out to us wherever you are and whenever you want. If you or anyone you know is interested in joining our team, we are waiting for you. There are no complicated interviews to trouble you. But just be sure to not overuse stack overflow for answers. Basic knowledge in HTML and CSS is enough to join our team. It's really great if you also know DBMS and Javascript.
